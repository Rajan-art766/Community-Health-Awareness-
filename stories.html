Ravi’s Journey to Fitness

Ravi, a 35-year-old teacher, turned his life around by adopting a healthy diet and walking daily. He lost 12 kg in 4 months and inspired his students to join him!

Anita’s Mental Health Awareness Drive

After facing anxiety, Anita started a local awareness campaign on mental health. Her sessions now help over 200 women in her neighborhood.

Blood Donors Club Initiative

A group of college students created a volunteer club to organize regular blood donation drives, saving countless lives through their dedication.
